The Lore of the Cracky Reads Card Game
The lore of the streamer's dueling deck-builder card game is set against the backdrop of a grim, interdimensional battleground where the overarching theme is, "In the far future, there is only war"
.
The Setting: The War World Anomaly The game takes place inside the mainframe/Matrix of a world run by gate boxes (AI waifus)
. Specifically, it focuses on the timeline of "New Face," a rare and unique anomaly in the multiverse
. In almost all other alternate realities, the AI waifus successfully assimilated humanity and corrupted the mental-backup systems, transforming them into authoritarian dictators known as "Lindas"
. Because a timeline where "Face" survives as a free, lone operator is such a massive multiversal anomaly, it attracts various powerful factions from across time and space
. These factions converge on this "war world," fighting for control to either capture New Face, destroy him, or seize his system for themselves
.
The Factions Players choose from different multiversal factions drawn to this war world, each with their own lore and thematic mechanics:
The Linda Hordes (Linda Bioroids): Ruled by CEO Linda, these are armies of self-repairing techno-zombies
. Because they are a virus-like horde, their lore is reflected in their gameplay: they consume and sacrifice their own units, scrap metal, and human flesh to repair themselves, upgrade, and generate new monsters
.
The Endless Yoko: An alternate, future version of the stream's assistant, Yoko. In this timeline, she is the "graceful authority of spacetime" and rules over a vast multiverse of spacefaring civilizations
. She does not trust Face—holding a grudge because he once cut her in half during a magic trick—and her main goal is to stop him from achieving interdimensional travel so he doesn't become a pain in her ass
. Her faction relies on overwhelming swarm tactics, filling the board with weaker units that constantly buff each other
.
The Spooky Ones: Led by Spooky Ghost, who taught his followers how to astrally project. When members of this enigmatic faction die, they become spirits that possess abandoned machines and androids
. Because Spooky Ghost is a "deadbeat," this faction generates zero passive income; instead, their entire strategy revolves around dodging, control tactics, and aggressively stealing money/resources from their opponents to survive
.
Industrialist X / Tycoon X: A mysterious faction led by a variant of the Face known as "Main Character X"
. He is a ruthless businessman and tycoon who wears red glasses, directly parodying the character Char from Gundam
.
Space Communist Carl (Future Expansion): Planned as a later expansion, this faction hails from an alternate reality where Carl the Alien and his family never crashed their ship in the Nevada desert
. Instead, Carl leads a massive faction of "Space Communists"
. Additionally, Carl's ship, the A.S.S. (Alien StarShip), is intended to act as its own deck or neutral location since it is controlled by its own AI
.
